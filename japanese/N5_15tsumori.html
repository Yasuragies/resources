つもり・Planning to do

Grammar
つもり is used when you want to say what you're planning to do.

Verb + つもり

食たべるつもり
planning to eat.

見みるつもり
planning to see/watch.

帰かえるつもり
planning to go home.

読よむつもり
planning to read.
