Suggestion


一緒に = いっしょに = together
Grammar
adding か after negative sentence will turn it into a suggestion/invitation to someone to do someothing
一緒に食たべませんか
why don't we eat together

一緒に見みませんか
why don't we see/watch together

一緒に寝ねませんか
why don't we sleep together

一緒に起おきません
why don't we wake up together

一緒に会あいません
why don't we meet together

一緒に待まちません
why don't we wait together

一緒に帰かえりしません why don't we go home together

一緒に読よみません
why don't we read together

一緒に遊あそびません
why don't we play together

一緒に死しにませんか
why don't we die together

一緒に聞ききませんか
why don't we listen together

一緒に行いきませんか
why don't we go together

話はなしませんか
why don't (we) speak together

Irregular verb

Example:


料理りょうりしませんか
not cook.

掃除そうじしませんか
not clean.

勉強べんきょうしませんか
not study.

ゲームべんきょうやりませんか
not study.
