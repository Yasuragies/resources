In this page we're going to learn how to use a verb, In japanese verb is devided into 3 category
1. Ichidan.
2. Godan.
3. Irregular.

But don't think too much about ichidan and godan now, let's call them normal verb for now.

so in this page we only need to concern ourself with
1. Normal verb.
2. Irregular verb.

before continue, we have to get ourself familiar with particle を which is a verb particle.
We usually use を in between of Noun and verb.

Noun を Verb

Example

私 は ラーメンを食たべる
I eat ramen.

Furthermore, these verb like 食べる are called dictionary form.
dictionary form itself doesn't have time indication, so it can means "eat" or "will eat" depending on the context.
but most of the time it will mean "will eat"

私 は ラーメンを食たべる
I eat ramen.

Can also mean

私 は ラーメンを食たべる
I will eat ramen.


as you know in Japenese we don't have to say the word 私 "I" over and over again, it will sounds unnatural.
so even if you don't say "I" the word that you're saying will automaticly refer to you or the person that are already in topic.

Example :


私 は ラーメンを食たべる
I will eat ramen.

and

ラーメンを食たべる
I will eat ramen.

both means the same.

More example :

曲きょくを聞きく
I (will) listen to a music.

ふみこ は 映画えいが を 見みる
Fumiko (will) watch a movie.

We've learned に particle before, but in this case we're going to meet them again but in different usage.
this time, に particle can work as a destination of our action, just like "to" in english.

so so far we know に particle can work as.
1. to mark the location where something exist.
2. to mark destination of our action.

Grammar :

noun に verb

in this case noun will become destination of verb

Example :

駅えきに 行いく (I will) go to the station.

たけし 先生せんせい に 話す (I will) Talk to Takeshi sensei.


For the next one, we're going to meet で particle, we can use this particle to mark the location where action takes place.

Comparison :

に particle :
to mark the location where something exist.

で particle :
to mark the location where action takes place.

Grammar :

A で verb

"A" become the location where the verb is happening.


Example :

喫茶店きっさてん で コーヒ を 飲のむ。 (will) Drink coffee at cafe.

史子ふみこ は 図書館としょかん で 本ほん を 読よむ。
Fumiko (will) reads book at the library.


Irregular verb

する = to do
やる = to do
来くる = to come

for する and やる it literally means to do in English but they have a different usage, but in general some noun can be attached with する and やる to turn it into a verb.

Example :

1. 勉強べんきょう = study → 勉強べんきょう する = to study.
2. 告白こくはく = confession → 告白こくはく する = to confess.
3. 洗濯せんたく = laundry → 洗濯せんたく する = to do laundry

Sentence example :

今日きょう は 日本語にほんご を 勉強べんきょう する
Today I will study japanese.
もりやま は 明日あした、部屋へや を 掃除そうじ する
Tomorrow　Moriyama will clean (his) room.
