Introduction to japanese verb conjugation.
In Japanese language, verbs are devided into 3 categories: Ichidan, Godan and irregular.
In this page we're going to learn how to conjugate ichidan, godan, and irregular verb into negative form.
a single verb can only fall into 1 category, either they're ichidan or godan.

Ichidan verb negative form conjugation.
Ichidan verb include:
食べる = たべる = to eat
見る = みる = to see/watch
寝る = ねる = to sleep
起きる = おきる = to wake up

to turn ichidan verb into negative form, replace the る in the end with ない　

食たべる -> 食たべない
eat -> not eat

見みる ->　見みない
see -> not see

寝ねる -> 寝ねない
sleep -> not sleep

起おきる -> 起おきない
wake up -> not wake up

Example

ふみこ は 、ラーメンを食べない
Fumiko (doesn't/will not) eat ramen

今週 は テレビ を 見ない
(I) (don't/will not) watch TV this week.

こばやし は 今夜、 寝ねい
Kobayashi (doesn't/will not) sleep tonight.

明日 は 起きない
(I) (will) not wake up tommorow.

Godan verb negative form conjugation
Godan verbs include:
会う = あう = to meet
待つ = まつ = to wait
帰る = かえる = to return home
読む = よむ = to read
遊ぶ = あそぶ = to play
死ぬ = しぬ = to die
聞く = きく = to listen
行く = いく = to go
話す = はなす = to speak


All the verbs that ended other than る is godan.

Careful. 帰る and 見る both ended with る but 帰る is godan and 見る is ichidan.
you must remember which one is godan and which one is ichidan.

Godan verbs are devided into 4 categories.

Verb that ended with う・つ・る
会う・待つ・帰る

Verb that ended with む・ぶ・ぬ
読む・遊ぶ・死ぬ

Verb that ended with く
聞く・行く

Verb that ended with す
話す

conjugate う・つ・る　to negative form.
 to conjugate this category into negative form, replace the　う・つ・る part。
う -> わない
つ -> たない
る -> らない

会あう -> 会あわない
meet -> (will) not meet

待まつ -> 待またない
wait -> (will) not wait

帰かえる -> 帰かえらない
return home -> (will) not return home

conjugate む・ぶ・ぬ　to　to negative form.
 to conjugate this category into negative form, replace the　む・ぶ・ぬ part。
む -> まない
ぶ -> ばない
ぬ -> なない

読よむ -> 読よまない
read -> (will) not read

遊あそぶ -> 遊あそばない
play -> (will) not play

死しぬ -> 死しなない
die -> (will) not die

conjugate く　into　to negative form.
 to conjugate く to negative form, replace く with かない

聞きく　-> 聞きかない
hear -> (will) not hear

行いく　-> 行いかない
go -> (will) not go

conjugate す　into negative form.
 to conjugate す to negative form, replace す with さない

話はなす　-> 話はなさない
speak -> (will) not speak

Example

来年 は ふみこ に 会わない。
Tommorow （I）（don't/will not) meet fumiko next year.

田中 は 音楽 を 聞かない
Tanaka (doesn't/will not) listen to a music.

田中 は 来月、この学校に行かない
Tanaka (doesn't/will not) go to this school next next month.

Irregular verb negative form conjugation
irregular verb include:
する = to do
やる = to do

Even though irregular verbs can stand alone, they're usually attached to noun like:
テニス = tenis
ゲーム = game
勉強 = べんきょう = study
宿題 = しゅくだい = homework
料理 = りょうり = cooking

テニスをやる = tenis = to do tennis.
ゲームをやる = game = to do gamming.
勉強する = べんきょうする = to do study.
宿題する= しゅくだいをする = to do homework.
料理する = りょうりする = to do cooking.

Note:
- やる is only limited to physical action only.
- Using を is optional.

To conjuage irregular verb into negative form:
する -> しない
to do -> will not do

やる -> やらない (やる is also a part of godan verb)
to do -> will not do

Example: ジュン は 今夜、勉強しない
jun (Doesn't/will not) tonight

かな は 今夜、料理をしない
Kana will not cook tonight.
